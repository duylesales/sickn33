🤖 Why Y Combinator is Changing its Model

Y Combinator (YC) is the undisputed kingmaker of Silicon Valley, famously seeding giants like Airbnb, Stripe, and Dropbox.

For over a decade, their investment thesis was incredibly consistent: find two elite computer science graduates from Stanford, lock them in a house for three months, and tell them to "make something people want." In the face of the generative AI revolution, YC has aggressively pivoted.
