🏢 The most dangerous moment for an AI startup? The first enterprise sales meeting.

Building an app with AI gets you a stunning prototype in days. But when the enterprise buyer hands you their 150-point Vendor Security Assessment Questionnaire (VSAQ)... your prototype fails immediately.

Enterprise IT doesn't care about your CSS. They care about:
✅ Single Sign-On (SAML/OIDC) so they can manage employee access.
✅ Hard Multi-Tenancy (Row Level Security) so data never leaks between clients.
✅ VPC Isolation so your database isn't sitting on the public internet.

Transitioning from an AI prototype to an enterprise-ready SaaS requires deep architectural changes.
