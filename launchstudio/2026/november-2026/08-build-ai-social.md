🏗️ You can build an AI app in a weekend. You can't un-build a bad architecture decision in a weekend. 

That asymmetry is what separates technical founders who scale from those who spend months rewriting code. 📉

When building AI apps, you are actually assembling 5 interconnected systems:
1️⃣ The AI Integration Layer (where your OpenAI key lives)
2️⃣ Data Architecture (Supabase with proper RLS)
3️⃣ Auth & Authorization (Magic links, OAuth, and RBAC)
4️⃣ Payment Processing (Stripe webhooks, not just a button)
5️⃣ Deployment & CI/CD Pipelines

A "fast and fragile" prototype looks identical to a "durable" app in a demo. But with real users, the fragile one leaks data and crashes. The durable one handles thousands of users gracefully.
