💸 The biggest threat to your AI startup is not competitors. It is your unit economics.

Traditional SaaS has 90% gross margins. AI SaaS has variable COGS. If you hardcode GPT-4o for every user interaction, your power users will bankrupt you.

Founders fall into 3 margin traps:
📉 Naive Chat History: Sending the same 20-message transcript to the API over and over.
📉 The "Over-Model": Using expensive GPT-4o for basic extraction tasks.
📉 Redundant Generation: Paying full price for an answer the AI generated yesterday.

You must architect for Margin Defense. Implement Multi-Model Routing, Semantic Caching, and Token Summarization.
