🚨 Your firewall is completely blind to AI vulnerabilities.

When you use an AI coding tool to build a SaaS, you get standard security: JWT, HTTPS, SQLi protection. 

But standard security cannot detect a Denial of Wallet (DoW) attack. It won't stop a hacker from sending massive 10,000-word prompts to your unsecured OpenAI endpoint until your corporate credit card maxes out.

In 2026, the biggest SaaS threats aren't SQL injections; they are:
💥 Denial of Wallet (DoW) Attacks
💥 Direct & Indirect Prompt Injections (hiding instructions in uploaded PDFs)
💥 RAG Data Leakage (AI accidentally revealing Company B's data to Company A)

Protecting your AI app requires database-level Row Level Security, Redis token rate limiters, and secure API proxies.
