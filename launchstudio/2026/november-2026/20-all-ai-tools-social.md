🔍 We evaluated all AI tools for app development. 
The verdict? The "One Tool to Rule Them All" doesn't exist.

Founders keep looking for a magical text box that spits out a production-ready, revenue-generating SaaS. 

Here is the uncomfortable truth:
- Bolt is amazing for UI, but has no persistent database.
- Lovable builds great React components, but connects insecurely to databases.
- Cursor is an incredible IDE, but won't architect your cloud infrastructure.

To reach production, you don't need one tool. You need a highly specific *stack*, plus human systems engineering where the AI stops.
