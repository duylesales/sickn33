# Social Media Post: Migrating from Firebase to Supabase for AI Startups

Firebase is the fastest way to build an AI prototype. It is also the fastest way to hit a wall when you go to production.

If you are building an AI SaaS, you will eventually need:
1️⃣ Native Vector Storage (`pgvector`) for RAG pipelines
2️⃣ Row-Level Security (RLS) for enterprise multi-tenancy
3️⃣ Predictable compute costs (instead of paying per document read)

Firestore's NoSQL model cannot handle this. The migration to Postgres (and Supabase) is inevitable.

We just published a step-by-step 4-week sprint guide on migrating your AI startup from Firebase to Supabase with zero downtime.
