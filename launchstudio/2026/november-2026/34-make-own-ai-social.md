💸 The most expensive mistake in AI SaaS is trying to "make your own AI".

Founders think defensibility means renting AWS GPUs and spending €40,000 fine-tuning an open-source model to learn their industry's facts. 

But fine-tuned models suffer from:
📉 Catastrophic Forgetting (learning your data destroys their general logic).
📉 The Un-Updateable Brain (if a fact changes, you must pay €40,000 to retrain).
📉 Dangerous Hallucinations (they confidently invent facts using your exact jargon).

The solution? A robust Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline. RAG stores facts securely in a vector database and feeds them to the LLM at inference time. Zero retraining. Perfect citations. Strict access controls.
