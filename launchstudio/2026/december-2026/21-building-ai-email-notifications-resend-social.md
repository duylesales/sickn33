# Social Media Post: Building AI-Powered Email Notifications with Resend

AI tasks take time. If you are generating a video or analyzing 500 PDFs with an agent, the user cannot sit on a loading screen for 20 minutes.

AI SaaS products require a decoupled, asynchronous UX. 
The pattern: "We are processing this in the background and will email you when it's done."

Don't use legacy email providers and wrestle with 1990s HTML `<table>` layouts.
Use Resend and React Email.

1️⃣ React Email lets you build email templates using standard React components and Tailwind CSS.
2️⃣ Resend integrates perfectly with Next.js, allowing you to pass those React components directly to their API.
3️⃣ Resend webhooks allow you to track bounces and protect your sender reputation automatically.

Treat your transactional emails as a core part of the AI UX.

Read the technical guide on the LaunchStudio blog.
