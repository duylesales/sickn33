# Social Media Post: Building an AI Changelog and Status Page for SaaS Transparency

Enterprise clients don't trust black boxes. 

If your RAG pipeline fails to parse a critical PDF, and your users are kept in the dark, you will lose the contract renewal.

To win B2B trust, you need radical transparency:
1️⃣ Automated Status Pages: Don't just ping `/api/health`. Create a health check that actually sends a dummy prompt through your entire LLM pipeline every minute. If it fails, auto-update your public status page to "Degraded."
2️⃣ Dependency Tracking: If OpenAI goes down, your status page should auto-flag it so your team doesn't take the blame.
3️⃣ Model Changelogs: Never "shadow update" your system prompts. If you switch from GPT-4 to Claude, document it in a `/changelog`. Users notice when the AI's "vibe" changes.

Transparency builds enterprise trust. Read our guide on the LaunchStudio blog.
