🚀 The End of the 40-Hour Work Week

The 40-hour work week is not a law of nature; it is an industrial-era artifact designed by Henry Ford in 1926 to optimize physical factory labor.

For decades, modern knowledge workers have been forced into this obsolete physical paradigm, resulting in massive inefficiencies and "performative working" (pretending to look busy).
