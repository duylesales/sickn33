🤖 Bootstrapping in the Age of AI

For a decade, the Silicon Valley narrative dictated that the only legitimate way to build a tech company was to grovel before Sand Hill Road Venture Capitalists, trade away 30% of your equity, and burn through millions of dollars to achieve "hyper-growth." This narrative was a trap.

In the AI era, the marginal cost of building software has plummeted to near zero, triggering a massive renaissance in **Bootstrapping**—the art of building highly profitable, un-funded Micro-SaaS empires.
