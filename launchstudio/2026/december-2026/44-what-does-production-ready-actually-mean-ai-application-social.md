🚨 "It worked every time I tested it." He was the ONLY person who ever tested it. That's not production-ready — that's a demo. 😱

"Production-ready" gets used constantly and means nothing without a checkable definition. Here's the real one — 4 pillars: 🧠

1️⃣ RELIABILITY — works under real concurrent usage, not just your solo testing
2️⃣ SECURITY — data isolated between customers, not just visually locked
3️⃣ SCALABILITY — appropriate to YOUR needs (not over-engineered, not under-built)
4️⃣ OPERABILITY — you know when it breaks, and can fix it without heroics

What it does NOT mean: feature-complete, "perfect," or enterprise-grade for 50 customers. 🎯

Why AI demos feel ready but usually aren't: never tested with 2+ simultaneous users, minimal security, zero monitoring plan — despite looking polished. 📉
