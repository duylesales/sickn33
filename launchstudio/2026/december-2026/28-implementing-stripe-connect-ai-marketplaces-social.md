# Social Media Post: Implementing Stripe Connect for AI Marketplace Platforms

If you are building an AI platform where users can create and sell their own custom AI Agents (like the GPT Store), you have a massive payments routing problem.

You cannot legally collect all the money into your startup's bank account and manually wire Excel payouts to creators every Friday. That is an AML/KYC disaster.

You need **Stripe Connect**.
1️⃣ Onboarding: Creators use Stripe's hosted UI to submit their ID and bank details (keeping sensitive data off your servers).
2️⃣ Destination Charges: When a buyer pays $100 to use an AI agent, Stripe splits the payment at the source. Your platform automatically keeps a $20 fee, and the $80 routes directly to the creator's bank account.

Don't hold other people's money. Let Stripe do it.
