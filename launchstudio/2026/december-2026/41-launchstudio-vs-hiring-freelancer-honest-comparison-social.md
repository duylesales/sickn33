🚨 An honest comparison article that ISN'T just marketing: when a freelancer actually beats LaunchStudio, and when it doesn't. 🤝

Freelancers are genuinely great for many founders. Here's when each option actually wins: 🧠

Freelancer wins when: ✅
✅ Ongoing, open-ended product development
✅ Highly specialized narrow expertise needed
✅ You want to learn to code alongside support
✅ Budget-flexible, timeline-flexible

LaunchStudio wins when: ✅
✅ You have a REAL deadline (launch, demo day, trade fair)
✅ Security/payments need production-grade rigor
✅ You want zero single-point-of-failure risk
✅ You don't want to manage an ongoing relationship

She used BOTH: **LaunchStudio** for her trade-fair deadline (fixed price, fixed timeline, backed by Manifera's 120+ engineers), then a freelancer for ongoing feature work afterward — made easy because LaunchStudio's code was fully documented. 🛡️

Result: hit her deadline, smooth handoff, zero friction. 🚀
