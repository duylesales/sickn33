🤖 GDPR and AI: Navigating the Regulatory Minefield

The core ethos of Machine Learning is "collect all data and remember everything forever." The core ethos of European Privacy Law (GDPR) is "collect the minimum data required and delete it upon request." These two philosophies are fundamentally opposed.

For B2B SaaS founders building AI features, navigating this contradiction is the difference between a successful European expansion and a €20 million regulatory fine.
