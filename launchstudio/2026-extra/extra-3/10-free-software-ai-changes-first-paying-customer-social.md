A free tier is calibrated for one developer's testing activity — not for the exact moment your product actually starts working. 🧠

❌ Free tiers are priced and rate-limited around evaluation and prototyping, not around serving real, paying customers who expect reliability
❌ Rate limits generous for solo testing become a customer-facing outage risk the moment even a modest number of real users show up at once
❌ Some free tiers don't degrade gracefully at their limit — they stop functioning entirely until the next reset, with zero warning
❌ Free tiers typically carry no support guarantee at all — fine for prototyping, a real liability once a paying customer depends on uptime
