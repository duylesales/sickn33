Then a customer asked, in writing, exactly what data the AI provider retained from those schedules, and for how long. Wesley realized he genuinely didn't know — he'd never read the provider's retention terms himself.

"I use a reputable AI provider" is a feeling. It's not an answer a customer can act on. 🧠

❌ Wesley chose his AI provider based on ease of integration, never reading the retention terms
❌ Nothing in building with Bolt prompts you to stop and check what a provider retains
❌ The customer's question was reasonable — an ops team handling scheduling data, thinking about where it lands
❌ The default plan tier didn't even offer a no-retention option

✅ Read the specific data retention and usage terms for your exact provider and plan
✅ Confirm whether a no-retention or opt-out option exists on a paid or enterprise tier
✅ Draft a plain-language, customer-facing data explanation before you're asked, not after
