🚨 An unplanned two-week hospital stay nearly took Marit Voskuijlen's entire company offline — a domain renewal failed silently, her database crept toward a storage limit, and not one other person on earth could log in to fix either. 🏥

🧠 "Bus factor" isn't about dying, it's about a bad month — and for most solo AI-native founders, the honest answer to "how many people keep this running" is one.

❌ Database, domain registrar, and Stripe account all lived under Marit's own personal logins, with zero second admin
❌ Founders moving fast with Lovable, Bolt, or Cursor click through setup screens using one email for everything — "add a second admin" always feels like a task for later
❌ A domain quietly expiring or a failed payment run just sits there unresolved if the one person who could fix it isn't reachable
❌ Unlike a code bug, there's no "fix it later" once a domain has actually lapsed or an account's been suspended

✅ List every account the product depends on — hosting, database, domain, payment provider, third-party API keys
✅ Add a second trusted admin directly to each service, not just a shared password
✅ Route billing and system alerts to two email addresses instead of one
