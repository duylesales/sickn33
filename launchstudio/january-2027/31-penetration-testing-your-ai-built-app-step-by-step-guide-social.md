## 📘 LinkedIn Post

**Penetration Testing Your AI-Built App: A Step-by-Step Guide**

AI-native founders are building prototypes faster than ever with tools like Lovable, Bolt, and Cursor. But the gap between "it works" and "it's launch-ready" is where most startups stumble.

Penetration testing is the most effective way to discover exploitable vulnerabilities in your AI-built application before malicious actors do. This step-by-step guide walks founders through the entire penetration testing process: scoping, reconnaissance, vulnerability scanning, manual exploitation, privilege escalation, and reporting. We highlight the specific attack vectors that are most commonly exploitable in AI-generated codebases and explain how LaunchStudio security team conducts pentests for AI-native founders.
