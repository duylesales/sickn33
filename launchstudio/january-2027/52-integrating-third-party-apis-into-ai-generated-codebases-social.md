## 📘 LinkedIn Post

**Integrating Third-Party APIs into AI-Generated Codebases: Common Pitfalls**

AI-native founders are building prototypes faster than ever with tools like Lovable, Bolt, and Cursor. But the gap between "it works" and "it's launch-ready" is where most startups stumble.

Third-party API integrations are where AI-generated code most frequently breaks in production. This article catalogs the most common integration pitfalls — including missing error handling, absent retry logic, hardcoded credentials, missing rate limit handling, and synchronous blocking calls — and provides production-grade patterns for each. We cover integrations with popular services like Stripe, SendGrid, Twilio, and OpenAI, and share the integration review checklist LaunchStudio uses during code audits.
