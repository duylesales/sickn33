## 📘 LinkedIn Post

**How to Implement Role-Based Access Control in Your AI-Generated App**

AI-native founders are building prototypes faster than ever with tools like Lovable, Bolt, and Cursor. But the gap between "it works" and "it's launch-ready" is where most startups stumble.

Role-Based Access Control is essential for any multi-user SaaS application, but AI-generated codebases rarely implement it correctly — or at all. This technical guide covers designing and implementing a robust RBAC system in your AI-built application, including role hierarchy design, permission matrices, middleware implementation, and frontend conditional rendering. We provide code examples compatible with common AI-generated architectures and share the RBAC patterns LaunchStudio engineers implement most frequently.
