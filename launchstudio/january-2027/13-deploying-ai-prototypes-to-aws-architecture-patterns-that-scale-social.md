## 📘 LinkedIn Post

**Deploying AI Prototypes to AWS: Architecture Patterns That Scale**

AI-native founders are building prototypes faster than ever with tools like Lovable, Bolt, and Cursor. But the gap between "it works" and "it's launch-ready" is where most startups stumble.

Deploying your AI-generated prototype to AWS requires thoughtful architecture decisions that balance cost, performance, and scalability. This technical guide covers the most effective AWS architecture patterns for AI-built SaaS applications, including serverless with Lambda, containerized with ECS/Fargate, and managed Kubernetes with EKS. We detail the pros, cons, and cost implications of each approach, drawing from LaunchStudio deployment playbook developed across dozens of founder engagements.

At LaunchStudio (by Manifera), we help founders bridge this gap with our proven 4-step process: Audit → Harden → Integrate → Deploy.
