## 📘 LinkedIn Post

**Building a Scalable Notification System for Your AI-Native SaaS**

AI-native founders are building prototypes faster than ever with tools like Lovable, Bolt, and Cursor. But the gap between "it works" and "it's launch-ready" is where most startups stumble.

A well-designed notification system drives user engagement and retention, but building one that scales is more complex than most founders expect. This technical guide covers designing and implementing a multi-channel notification system — email, push notifications, in-app alerts, and SMS — for your AI-built SaaS product. We cover notification preferences, delivery guarantees, template management, and integration with services like SendGrid, OneSignal, and Knock. LaunchStudio shares their notification architecture blueprint used across client projects.
