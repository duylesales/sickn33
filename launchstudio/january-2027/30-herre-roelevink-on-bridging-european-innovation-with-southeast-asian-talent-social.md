**Herre Roelevink on Bridging European Innovation with Southeast Asian Talent**

AI-native founders are building prototypes faster than ever with tools like Lovable, Bolt, and Cursor. But the gap between "it works" and "it's launch-ready" is where most startups stumble.

Manifera founder and director Herre Roelevink shares his insights on building a successful bridge between European innovation and Southeast Asian development talent.
