## 📘 LinkedIn Post

**Microservices vs Monolith: The Right Architecture for Your AI MVP**

AI-native founders are building prototypes faster than ever with tools like Lovable, Bolt, and Cursor. But the gap between "it works" and "it's launch-ready" is where most startups stumble.

The microservices vs monolith debate takes on new dimensions when applied to AI-generated codebases. This article provides a decision framework specifically designed for AI-native founders, considering factors like team size, expected traffic, feature complexity, and deployment velocity. We argue that for most AI-built MVPs, a well-structured monolith is the right starting point and explain the specific indicators that signal when it is time to extract microservices. LaunchStudio architecture team shares their migration playbook for founders who have outgrown their monolith.

At LaunchStudio (by Manifera), we help founders bridge this gap with our proven 4-step process: Audit → Harden → Integrate → Deploy.
