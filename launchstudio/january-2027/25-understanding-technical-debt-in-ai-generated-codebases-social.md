**Understanding Technical Debt in AI-Generated Codebases**

AI-native founders are building prototypes faster than ever with tools like Lovable, Bolt, and Cursor. But the gap between "it works" and "it's launch-ready" is where most startups stumble.

AI-generated code introduces a unique form of technical debt that differs from traditional development debt. This article categorizes the five types of technical debt commonly found in AI-generated codebases — structural, security, performance, dependency, and architectural — and provides frameworks for measuring and prioritizing debt reduction. We also share LaunchStudio technical debt scoring system that helps founders understand the true state of their AI-generated code.
