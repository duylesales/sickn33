# Mastering Security Hardening for Next.js 14 App Router in AI SaaS

In 2027, building an AI-native SaaS isn't just about features; it's about robust infrastructure. Integrating Next.js 14 App Router brings immense power, but focusing on security hardening is what separates a prototype from a profitable business.

## The Challenge
Malicious actors exploit vulnerabilities in default configurations. When scaling an AI application, this issue compounds, causing frustrating bottlenecks and potential revenue loss.

## The Solution
Implement strict access controls, sanitize inputs, and regularly audit dependencies.

By standardizing this approach within your Next.js 14 App Router stack, you drastically reduce technical debt and build a foundation ready for enterprise clients.
