# Mastering Error Monitoring & Logging for Next.js 14 App Router in AI SaaS

In 2027, building an AI-native SaaS isn't just about features; it's about robust infrastructure. Integrating Next.js 14 App Router brings immense power, but focusing on error monitoring & logging is what separates a prototype from a profitable business.

## The Challenge
Silent failures in production ruin the customer experience before you even know about them. When scaling an AI application, this issue compounds, causing frustrating bottlenecks and potential revenue loss.

## The Solution
Integrate structured logging and real-time alerting using Sentry or Datadog.

By standardizing this approach within your Next.js 14 App Router stack, you drastically reduce technical debt and build a foundation ready for enterprise clients.
