## 📘 LinkedIn Post

**Why Every AI-Built App Needs a Dedicated Error Monitoring Strategy**

AI-native founders are building prototypes faster than ever with tools like Lovable, Bolt, and Cursor. But the gap between "it works" and "it's launch-ready" is where most startups stumble.

AI-generated code introduces error patterns that are fundamentally different from hand-written code, making traditional error monitoring approaches insufficient. This article explains why AI-built applications need specialized error monitoring strategies, covers the best tools for the job (Sentry, LogRocket, Datadog), and provides a practical implementation guide. We also share the error monitoring playbook that LaunchStudio deploys for every AI-native client to ensure issues are caught and resolved before they impact users.
