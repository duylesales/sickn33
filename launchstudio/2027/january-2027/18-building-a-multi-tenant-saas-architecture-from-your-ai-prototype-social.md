**Building a Multi-Tenant SaaS Architecture from Your AI Prototype**

AI-native founders are building prototypes faster than ever with tools like Lovable, Bolt, and Cursor. But the gap between "it works" and "it's launch-ready" is where most startups stumble.

Most AI-generated prototypes are built as single-tenant applications, but production SaaS products require multi-tenant architecture for scalability and cost efficiency. This technical guide explains the key architectural decisions involved in converting a single-tenant AI prototype to a multi-tenant system, including database isolation strategies, tenant-aware authentication, data partitioning, and billing per tenant.
