## 📘 LinkedIn Post

**Why Your AI Prototype Authentication System Needs a Complete Overhaul**

AI-native founders are building prototypes faster than ever with tools like Lovable, Bolt, and Cursor. But the gap between "it works" and "it's launch-ready" is where most startups stumble.

Authentication is the most critical security component of any SaaS application, yet AI-generated authentication systems consistently contain dangerous vulnerabilities. This article exposes the most common authentication flaws in AI-built applications — including weak password hashing, missing rate limiting, JWT misconfigurations, and absent multi-factor authentication — and provides a systematic approach to replacing them with production-grade auth solutions using Auth0, Clerk, or NextAuth.

At LaunchStudio (by Manifera), we help founders bridge this gap with our proven 4-step process: Audit → Harden → Integrate → Deploy.
