🚀 **Decentralized Finance (DeFi) vs Traditional Banking**

Furthermore, cross-border settlements take days and extract massive rent-seeking fees. **Decentralized Finance (DeFi)** seeks to completely obliterate this infrastructure by replacing the human banker with an immutable, autonomous piece of blockchain code called a Smart Contract.
