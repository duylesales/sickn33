## What is an AI Assist Tool?

An **ai assist** tool (like Cursor, Lovable, or Bolt) acts as your technical co-founder. Instead of typing syntax, you type instructions. "Create a dashboard with a sidebar," or "Add a user login screen." The AI assist translates your intent into functional React or Python code.

### The Steps to Build an App with AI

1.  **Define the Scope:** Clearly articulate what you want to build. 
2.  **Prompting:** Use natural language to guide the AI to generate the UI and logic.
3.  **Iteration:** Refine the app by telling the AI what to change.
