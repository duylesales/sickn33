FAQ
What does it cost?
Most projects fall between €800 and €7,500. You always receive a fixed quote after a short call first. No surprises.
How can it be so affordable?
We work with senior Vietnamese engineers with 11+ years of enterprise experience (clients include Vodafone, TNO, CFLW). You communicate in English or Dutch, we handle the rest. And because your frontend already exists, we only fix what needs fixing.
How long does it take?
Timelines depend on scope, but most projects ship faster than you’d expect. After your intro call you receive a quote with an exact timeline.
Does my code stay mine?
Yes. All code lives in your repository, on your accounts. Full ownership is standard.
Can I keep building myself after?
Absolutely. We deliver documented, AI-readable code that works with Lovable, Cursor and Bolt. Keep building yourself or come back to us.
Do you handle hosting too?
Yes. Our Launch & Grow includes managed hosting, SSL, monitoring, backups and security updates. You don’t have to think about anything.
