## Why it Matters

Failing to establish high engineering standards early in the project leads to major scalability bottlenecks. This guide breaks down the core execution steps required to successfully handle optimizing cloud hosting costs and serverless utilization for your product.

### Strategic Execution Steps

To ensure success, engineering managers should focus on the following core areas:

1. **Track Resource Utilization:** Set up detailed cost tracking for cloud hosting, third-party APIs, and compute resource spend.
2. **Identify Wastage:** Periodically audit unused serverless execution time, oversized databases, and redundant environments.
3. **Leverage Cost-Effective Talent:** Balance your core in-house architect team with a dedicated remote team in Vietnam to optimize cost-per-feature.
