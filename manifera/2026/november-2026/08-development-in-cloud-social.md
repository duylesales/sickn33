A "Lift and Shift" cloud migration is financial suicide. ☁️💀

Taking an old, monolithic application from an on-premise server and just copy-pasting it into an AWS EC2 instance doesn't make it "Cloud Native." It makes it an incredibly expensive monolith hosted on someone else's computer.

To realize the economic promises of the cloud, you must refactor your codebase into serverless functions or horizontally scalable Kubernetes clusters. Otherwise, you're just paying a massive premium for legacy tech.
