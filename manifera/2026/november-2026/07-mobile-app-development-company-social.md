Is a tiny bug fix forcing your users to re-download your entire mobile app? 📲📉

The App Store review process is notoriously slow. If you deploy a critical hotfix, it can take 48 hours for Apple to approve it, leaving your users stranded with a broken app.

Modern Mobile DevOps fixes this completely. By using Over-The-Air (OTA) updates (like Microsoft CodePush), you can instantly beam JavaScript payload updates directly to the user's phone the next time they open the app. Zero App Store reviews. Immediate bug resolution.
