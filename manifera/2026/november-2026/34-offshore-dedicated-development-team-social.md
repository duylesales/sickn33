Giving offshore freelancers VPN access to your staging database is a SOC2 nightmare. 🔒😱

If they are using personal, unpatched laptops on public Wi-Fi, malware can tunnel directly into your network. Or worse, they can download your entire proprietary codebase.

Zero-Trust engineering is mandatory. Use Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (VDI). The offshore developer logs into a secure cloud VM. The code never touches their local hard drive, and clipboard/USB access is mathematically blocked.
