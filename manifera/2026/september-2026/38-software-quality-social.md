📐 **Software Quality is not an opinion. It is a strictly quantified, mathematical state.**

A CTO asks an agency how they ensure **software quality**. The agency replies, *"We have senior devs and we do a lot of manual QA testing!"*
The CTO instantly fires them.

Manual QA is a massive red flag. It optimizes for the "Happy Path" and is completely blind to backend structural flaws. 

Elite engineering teams do not rely on humans to test code. They replace subjective opinions with mathematical enforcement pipelines:
✅ **DORA Metrics:** Prove your deployment frequency and MTTR mathematically.
✅ **Cyclomatic Complexity:** CI/CD pipelines (SonarQube) that automatically reject Pull Requests if a function is too large or convoluted. 
✅ **Shift-Left SAST:** The pipeline reads the raw syntax and mathematically blocks the deployment if an OWASP vulnerability is detected.
