📉 **AI coding tools will not let you fire half your engineering team.**

A CEO reads a McKinsey report: "Generative AI increases developer productivity by 40%." The CEO buys GitHub Copilot and tells the VP of Engineering to reduce the offshore headcount by 40%.

The VP refuses. Why? Because the CEO misunderstands technical debt. 
**AI tools for software development** do not replace engineers. They replace *typing*.

If you leave a junior developer unsupervised with Copilot, they will generate unmaintainable, architecturally flawed code 40% faster. The AI doesn't know your GDPR rules. It doesn't know that its suggested database query will crash under 1 million rows.

*"If an engineer doesn't understand the AI code they just merged, they cannot debug it at 2:00 AM."*

The role of the engineer is shifting from Code Writer to **Code Reviewer**. Because AI generates massive volumes of code, you need *stronger* senior governance to audit it, not fewer engineers.
