🛑 **Beware of 'Resume-Driven Development' in offshore agencies.**

You hire an agency for **software outsourcing** to build a simple internal portal for 50 employees. Their proposal pitches: *Kubernetes, Kafka event streaming, GraphQL, and Microservices.*

It sounds impressive. But you just bought a Ferrari engine for a golf cart.

This is "Resume-Driven Development" (RDD). Cheap offshore agencies want to train their junior staff on complex enterprise tools so they can charge higher rates later. They use your simple app as a paid training ground, leaving you with a codebase your internal IT team cannot possibly maintain.

*"Complexity is a liability, not an asset. Complexity must be earned through scale."*
