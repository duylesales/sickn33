🏗️ **Trying to deeply customize a White-Label app is architectural suicide.**

A telemedicine CEO buys a generic white-label **app solution** for speed. It works great for 3 months. Then, they decide to build a custom AI triage feature to beat competitors. 

The devs realize the white-label source code is a rigid monolith. To add the AI feature, they have to write fragile "Monkey Patches" (hacks) to bypass the core logic. 
A month later, the vendor issues a mandatory security update. It overwrites the custom hacks, and the app goes down for three days.

This is the White-Label Customization Trap. White-label is great for MVPs. But the moment you need proprietary business logic, you are building your entire company on structural technical debt.
