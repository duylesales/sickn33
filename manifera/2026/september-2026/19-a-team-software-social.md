🦸‍♂️ **If your software relies on a "Hero Developer," you have a broken architecture.**

Every mid-sized tech company has a "Dave." Dave is brilliant. When the database crashes at 2:00 AM, Dave fixes it in 10 minutes. When a feature is late, Dave writes 5,000 lines of code over the weekend to save the launch.

The CEO loves Dave. The CTO should be terrified of Dave.

Dave is a catastrophic single point of failure. If Dave gets hit by a bus (or quits), your engineering velocity drops to zero because Dave hoards knowledge and writes "clever" undocumented code that no one else can read.

Elite architecture is boring, predictable, and maintainable by any competent engineer. You must enforce **a team software** capability:
✅ **Mandatory Pair Programming** for core infrastructure.
✅ **No Approval, No Merge:** CI/CD must mathematically block PRs unless two other engineers understand and approve the code.
