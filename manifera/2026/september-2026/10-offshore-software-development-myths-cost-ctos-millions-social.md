🔥 **6 offshore development myths that cost European CTOs millions:**

After 11 years running Dutch-Vietnamese engineering teams, here's what's actually true:

❌ **"Offshore code is low quality"**
✅ Code quality = engineering culture (reviews, testing, CI/CD), NOT geography. Ask to see their pipeline, not their location.

❌ **"Communication across time zones doesn't work"**
✅ You need 2-3h sync overlap. Everything else should be async anyway. NL↔Vietnam gives you 4-5 hours daily.

❌ **"Your IP is at risk"**
✅ IP risk is contractual, not geographical. Dutch law + your own repo + individual NDAs = same protection as hiring locally.

❌ **"Offshore is only for simple CRUD apps"**
✅ Top Vietnamese teams build fintech platforms, IoT systems, and AI products with Kubernetes, GraphQL, and microservices.

❌ **"You need constant oversight"**
✅ A mature partner has their own tech leads and PMs. Your role = set priorities + review architecture. Same as in-house.

❌ **"Choose the cheapest option"**
✅ €15/hour ≠ same service at a lower price. It's DIFFERENT service: junior devs, no reviews, no testing. The failed €50K project costs more than paying €35/hour upfront.

The formula that works: European governance + Vietnamese engineering mastery.
