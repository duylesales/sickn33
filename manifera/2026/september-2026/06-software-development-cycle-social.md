🧪 **You cannot test quality into a product at the end of the pipeline.**

In the traditional **software development cycle**, developers write code on Friday, throw it over the wall to QA on Monday, and wait for the bug reports. 

This "QA Handoff" is a massive financial bottleneck. A bug found during coding costs €100 to fix. A bug found during QA staging costs €1,000 because of the context-switching penalty. 

Elite engineering teams do not rely on manual QA to catch logic errors. They "Shift Left":
✅ **Test-Driven Development (TDD):** The developer writes the test *before* the code, mathematically proving the logic works from Day 1.
✅ **CI/CD Automation:** Automated pipelines run 1,000 tests on every commit, blocking regressions instantly. 
✅ **Exploratory Testing:** Human QA stops acting as a machine compiler and focuses on breaking the UX in unpredictable, human ways.

At Manifera, our Dutch Tech Leads enforce a strict Shift Left mentality on our offshore pods. We do not throw bugs over the wall. We engineer quality into the foundation.
