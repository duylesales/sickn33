🤔 **Staff augmentation or dedicated team? The wrong choice costs you 6 months.**

Two startups walk into a dev firm. Both need engineers. One picks staff augmentation. The other picks a dedicated team. Six months later, one is shipping features. The other is drowning in coordination overhead.

The difference? Matching the model to your growth stage.

**📌 Staff Augmentation = Right when:**
→ You have a strong CTO who can manage externals
→ You need specific skills for 3-6 months
→ Adding 1-3 devs to an existing, mature process

**📌 Dedicated Team = Right when:**
→ No internal tech leadership (or CTO is stretched thin)
→ You need 12-24 months of sustained capacity
→ Complex work spanning frontend + backend + infra
→ Team of 4-8+ engineers

**The Growth Stage Cheat Sheet:**
🌱 Pre-Seed: Dedicated team (2-3 devs) — need turnkey execution
🌿 Seed: Staff augmentation (1-2 devs) — CTO manages directly
🌳 Series A: Dedicated team (4-6 devs) — CTO should strategize, not manage tasks
🏢 Series B+: Hybrid — core team + augmented specialists

💰 Hidden cost difference: Staff aug requires 8-12h/week of YOUR management time. Dedicated teams? 3-5h/week.
