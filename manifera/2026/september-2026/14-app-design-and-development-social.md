🎨 **Design dictates the Database. If you design without an Architect, you are writing checks your backend can't cash.**

A startup founder pays a designer to create a beautiful, 50-screen Figma prototype. They hand it to an engineering agency and say, "Build this."

The Lead Architect looks at a simple "Live Global Search" bar on the dashboard and says, *"To make this load in under 3 seconds, we have to rewrite your entire database schema and set up Elasticsearch. That search bar alone costs €15,000."*

This is the "Design-First Fallacy." In Figma, adding a complex data widget takes 30 seconds. In engineering, it takes 3 weeks of complex backend architecture. 

Elite teams enforce **Architecture-Led Design**:
✅ The Data Schema is defined *before* the first pixel is drawn.
✅ If a UI widget requires an unscalable database join, the design is altered immediately.
