🌍 **Your team spans 3 time zones. Your standup process was designed for 1 office. Here's how to fix it.**

Stop trying to find a time that "works for everyone." It doesn't exist when you're spread across 6+ hours. Instead, use the Two-Tier System:

**Tier 1: Async Daily Update (EVERYONE)**
→ Post in dedicated Slack channel within 30 min of starting work:
✅ Yesterday: [tasks completed]
🔄 Today: [planned work]
🚧 Blockers: [what's stuck]
→ MAX 5 lines. Tag people for blockers. Use threads.

**Tier 2: Sync Standup (CRITICAL PATH ONLY)**
→ 15-min video call during overlap window
→ Only engineers collaborating on the same feature
→ Skip it on days with zero blockers in async updates

**For Amsterdam ↔ Ho Chi Minh City:**
→ Overlap window: 9:00-13:00 CET = 15:00-19:00 VN
→ 4 solid hours daily for real-time collaboration

💡 Sprint ceremonies (planning, review, retro) happen bi-weekly during overlap window. Everything else? Async.

Tools that work: Slack (async), Loom (async video), Linear (tasks), Google Meet (sync)
