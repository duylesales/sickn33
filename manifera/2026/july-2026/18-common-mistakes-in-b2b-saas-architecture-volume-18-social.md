❌ **Ignoring RBAC:** Hardcoding simple `is_admin` roles fails when enterprise clients demand custom permissions and SSO (SAML). 
❌ **The Monolithic Nightmare:** Don't tangle billing, auth, and core logic. Build a "modular monolith" so you can decouple into microservices later.
