📋 **The 90-Day Playbook for building a dedicated development team:**

Most dedicated team engagements fail not because of bad engineers — but because of bad onboarding. Here's the blueprint:

**Days 1-14: Partner Selection**
→ Define exact roles ("Senior React + Node.js", not "3 developers")
→ Interview ACTUAL engineers, not sample profiles
→ Sign monthly retainer with replacement SLA

**Days 15-28: Pre-Onboarding**
→ Document your dev environment setup (no "ask Dave, he knows")
→ Write a 5-10 page architecture overview
→ Pre-provision ALL access: GitHub, Jira, Slack, VPN, staging

**Days 29-56: The Onboarding Sprint** ← THE MOST CRITICAL PHASE
→ Daily 1-hour architecture walkthroughs
→ Pair programming on low-risk fixes
→ Assign buddies from your existing team
→ Every hour invested here saves 10 hours of rework later

**Days 57-90: First Production Release**
→ Assign a feature with real user impact
→ Full pipeline: dev → review → staging → QA → prod
→ Retrospective: what worked, what didn't

⚠️ Common mistakes:
❌ Treating them as freelancers (include in all-hands!)
❌ Skipping onboarding investment
❌ Expecting Day-1 parity with your 3-year veteran
