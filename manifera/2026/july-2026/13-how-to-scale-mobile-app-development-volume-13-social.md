📱 **Scaling a mobile app from 2 developers to 15? Prepare for chaos.**

Launching a mobile MVP is hard. Scaling the engineering team without breaking the codebase is harder. If you add more developers to a monolithic mobile app, you'll be drowning in merge conflicts and buggy App Store releases.

How elite engineering teams scale mobile development efficiently:
🔹 **Adopt a Monorepo:** Break your app into distinct, isolated modules (using Nx/Lerna). Let different pods work independently without breaking the core app.
🔹 **Automate Mobile CI/CD:** Stop wasting developer time manually uploading builds to TestFlight. Utilize Fastlane or Bitrise to automate linting, testing, and distribution.
🔹 **Hybrid Offshore Scaling:** Bypass local hiring bottlenecks by integrating dedicated remote pods in Southeast Asia to instantly double your engineering velocity.
