Choosing the right IT outsourcing model in 2026:

1️⃣ **Staff Augmentation ("Body Leasing")**
You rent devs to join your internal team. You manage them.
✅ Great for: Mature engineering teams needing a 3-month skill injection.
❌ Bad for: Startups without a technical leader. The devs will sit idle.

2️⃣ **Project-Based (Fixed Price)**
You hand over a spec. The agency builds it for €X by Date Y.
✅ Great for: Static websites, isolated API connectors.
❌ Bad for: Core B2B SaaS. If users want a pivot in month 2, you are trapped in "Change Request" contract hell. Agile dies.

3️⃣ **Dedicated Teams (Hub & Spoke)**
You hire an intact squad (Devs + QA + Scrum Master). You manage the backlog, they manage the execution. 
✅ Great for: Long-term core product development. High flexibility + deep domain knowledge.

**Pro-Tip:** Don't mix offshore and onshore devs randomly. Give the Dedicated Team a specific domain to own. Integrate via APIs, not 3-hour Zoom calls.
