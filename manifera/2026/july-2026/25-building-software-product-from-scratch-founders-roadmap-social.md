🚀 Non-technical founders lose €50K-€200K on average.

Not because their idea is bad.

Because they skip validation and jump straight to development.

Here's the roadmap that prevents this:

**Phase 0: Validate (< €500)**
Before ONE line of code:
→ Landing page + waitlist (€50-200)
→ 10 customer interviews (free)
→ Fake door test (€200-500 in ads)
→ Deliver the service MANUALLY first

The last one is the most powerful and least used.

**Phase 1: Define MVP scope**
Write every feature you want.
Then cut ruthlessly:

❌ Does it directly solve the core problem? No → CUT
❌ Will users pay WITHOUT this feature? Yes → CUT
❌ Can you manually handle it for first 100 users? Yes → CUT V1

Most founders go from 40 features to 6.
Instagram launched with 3.

**Phase 2: Choose your dev partner**
→ Tech co-founder: equity-for-leadership trade
→ Freelancer: <€15K, simple scope only
→ Dev company: full team, PM, QA included

**Phase 3: Build MVP (8-12 weeks)**
Realistic costs (offshore):
Simple: €15K-35K
Medium: €35K-70K
Complex: €70K-150K

**Phase 4: Launch to 20-50 users**
Don't add features. Fix friction.
Day-7 retention >40%? Scale.
Below 20%? Talk to churned users.

The 5 mistakes that burn cash fastest:

1️⃣ Building before validating
2️⃣ Over-specifying the MVP
3️⃣ Choosing cheapest developer
4️⃣ Ignoring mobile
5️⃣ No analytics from day one

Validate → Build small → Learn → Scale.
