🚧 **If QA is just a phase at the end of your sprint, it is destroying your feature velocity.**

When developers write code for three weeks and then throw it over the wall to manual testers, you create a massive bottleneck. Quality cannot be tested into a product at the end.

The Strategic Approach to Quality Engineering:
1️⃣ **The Shift-Left Methodology:** QA must start before coding begins. QA engineers write acceptance criteria alongside Product Managers to prevent architectural bugs early.
2️⃣ **Elevate QA to SDET:** Stop hiring manual "click-testers." Hire Software Development Engineers in Test (SDETs) who write complex automation code using Playwright or Cypress.
3️⃣ **DevQAOps:** Make automated testing the gatekeeper of your CI/CD pipeline. Unstable code physically cannot merge to production.
