🛡️ The #1 fear of Enterprise Compliance regarding Offshore Dev:
"We are giving devs 10,000km away access to our source code. How do we prevent a breach?"

The old way: Cameras watching developers in physical "clean rooms." (Obsolete in the remote-work era).

The 2026 way: **Zero Trust Architecture.**

You can't control their physical café, but you control the digital environment.

**The Offshore Security Playbook:**
1️⃣ **VDI (Virtual Desktops):** For highly sensitive IP. Code never touches their local laptop. It stays on European cloud servers. They just stream the screen.
2️⃣ **MDM Laptops:** Remote-wipe capabilities, enforced disk encryption. A stolen laptop = zero data loss.
3️⃣ **Automated Secret Scanning:** CI/CD blocks any commit containing an API key. 
4️⃣ **Data Anonymization:** Offshore devs NEVER see live customer data. Staging DBs must use synthetic, masked data (GDPR requirement).

**The ultimate vendor test? ISO 27001.**
If your offshore agency doesn't have it, words mean nothing. ISO 27001 proves an external auditor verified their background checks, access controls, and network security.

Build globally. Secure locally.
