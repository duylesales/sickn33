📈 **Calculating the ROI of React Native for Enterprise Apps.**

Why are CTOs abandoning dual-native (Swift/Kotlin) development for React Native frameworks? It comes down to pure economics and feature velocity.

The tangible financial benefits:
✅ **30-40% Cost Reduction:** One JavaScript/TypeScript codebase powers both iOS and Android. You no longer need to hire two separate, expensive engineering teams.
✅ **Accelerated Time-to-Market:** "Hot Reloading" and a massive open-source ecosystem allow you to launch your MVP months earlier, capturing market share faster.
✅ **Halved Maintenance Burden:** A single bug fix updates both platforms instantly, drastically lowering your Total Cost of Ownership (TCO).

Coupled with an elite dedicated offshore team, React Native is the most capital-efficient way to dominate the mobile landscape.
