🗺️ **Nearshore, offshore, onshore — these words have become meaningless. Here's the actual decision framework:**

Forget geography labels. Focus on 4 factors:

**💰 Factor 1: Cost**
→ Onshore (NL): €500K-650K/year for 5 devs
→ Nearshore (Poland): €200K-300K (0.4-0.5×)
→ Offshore (Vietnam): €90K-150K (0.2-0.3×)

**📞 Factor 2: Communication**
→ Onshore: 8h sync/day, native language
→ Nearshore: 6-8h, near-native
→ Offshore Vietnam: 4-5h overlap with CET — enough for all Agile ceremonies

**⚖️ Factor 3: Compliance**
→ The trick: choose an offshore partner with a European legal entity
→ Dutch contract law + EU DPA = same legal protection as onshore

**👥 Factor 4: Scalability**
→ NL: Small pool, 3-5 months to hire, high poaching
→ Vietnam: 530K+ devs, 2-3 weeks to scale, stable retention

**The Decision:**
✅ Onshore: Classified projects, hardware integration
✅ Nearshore: Strict language/timezone needs, moderate budget
✅ Offshore + EU governance: Cost priority, scale needs, 4-5h sync is sufficient

The optimal 2026 answer for most EU companies: **hybrid offshore with European governance.**
