🛍️ For 10 years, e-commerce meant monolithic platforms (Magento, traditional WordPress).

Change a button? The dev touches core PHP.
Traffic spike? The whole massive server cluster scales up.

In 2026, the Enterprise standard is **Headless CMS (MACH Architecture).**
Decouple the "body" (the database/backend) from the "head" (the frontend).

**Why Enterprise is moving Headless:**
⚡ **Performance:** The frontend is pre-rendered static HTML distributed globally via CDNs. Sub-100ms load times. Massive SEO boost.
📱 **Omnichannel:** The CMS spits out pure JSON via API. The exact same content feeds your Next.js website, your Flutter mobile app, and your in-store kiosk.
🛡️ **Security:** The public website doesn't connect to a database. It connects to read-only APIs. Attack surface drops by 90%.

**The Catch:** Flexibility breeds complexity. 
A monolith gives you everything in a box. 
Headless means you are orchestrating APIs (Contentful for text, Shopify Plus for cart, Algolia for search). Initial builds cost 30-50% more.

Headless is not for small merchants. It’s for €10M+ businesses where lightning-fast load times translate directly to massive conversion spikes.
