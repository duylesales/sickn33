🚨 **Are you using real production data in your staging environments?**

If your QA team clones production databases to test features, a breach in your loosely-secured staging environment exposes real PII. The result? Catastrophic GDPR fines.

In 2026, Quality Assurance and Cybersecurity cannot exist in silos. QA Security must be fully integrated. 
CTOs must enforce:
1️⃣ **Strict Data Masking:** Test data must be procedurally generated or completely anonymized. 
2️⃣ **Automated SAST/DAST:** Don't wait for an annual pentest. Automate static and dynamic vulnerability scanning in your CI/CD pipeline (DevSecOps).
3️⃣ **Security Acceptance Criteria:** A feature isn't "Done" unless it passes security tests during the sprint.
