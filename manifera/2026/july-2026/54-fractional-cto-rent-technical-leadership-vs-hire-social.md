👔 Non-technical founder dilemma:
You raised a Seed round. Investors say, "Get a CTO."

Option A: Hire a €200k/year executive + 3% equity. (Burns your runway).
Option B: Promote your best freelance dev to CTO. (They don't know how to build a scalable org, tech debt explodes).

The 2026 standard? **The Fractional CTO.**

Rent 20% of the time of a €200k executive for €4k-€8k/month. Zero equity.

**3 times you MUST rent instead of buy:**
1️⃣ **The Agency Overseer:** You outsourced the MVP. The fractional CTO validates the architecture so you don't get taken advantage of.
2️⃣ **The Investor Whisperer:** Prepping for Series A. They pass the technical due diligence audit for you.
3️⃣ **The Team Scaler:** Growing from 4 devs to 10. They implement the Agile framework and CI/CD pipelines so things don't break.

**When NOT to use a Fractional CTO:**
❌ DeepTech/AI startups where the algorithm is the entire business.
❌ When you just need someone to write React code faster. Fractional CTOs don't code; they strategize.

Pair a European Fractional CTO with an Offshore Dev Team = Maximum alignment + Asian economics.
