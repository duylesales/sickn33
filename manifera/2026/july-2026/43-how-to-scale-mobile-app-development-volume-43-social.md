📱 **Putting 30 developers into a single mobile codebase is a recipe for Git merge chaos.**

Scaling a mobile app team from 3 to 30 engineers breaks traditional monolithic architectures. You'll spend 80% of your time fixing regressions instead of building features.

How to truly scale enterprise mobile development:
1️⃣ **Micro-Frontends:** Break your app into independent modules (Auth, Payments). Autonomous pods can build features simultaneously without stepping on each other's toes.
2️⃣ **Standardize on React Native:** Stop scaling two separate, expensive Swift and Kotlin teams. Unify your engineering around a single JavaScript codebase.
3️⃣ **Automate Mobile CI/CD:** Don't let a developer manually compile the `.ipa` file. Use Bitrise or Fastlane to auto-distribute builds on every commit.
