📱 **An enterprise mobile app is not just a coding exercise; it is a strategic business platform.**

If you treat mobile development tactically—just hiring coders and handing them designs—you end up with a bloated app that crashes constantly and drains your maintenance budget.

The Strategic Blueprint for Mobile Success:
1️⃣ **Cross-Platform Supremacy:** Stop building twice. Use React Native to deploy high-performance apps to iOS and Android from a single JavaScript codebase.
2️⃣ **API-First Architecture:** The app shouldn't execute heavy database queries. Decouple it entirely from your backend using lightweight, optimized GraphQL endpoints.
3️⃣ **Offline Resilience:** Field workers operate in dead zones. Build local databases (WatermelonDB) so the app functions offline and syncs seamlessly when connectivity returns.
