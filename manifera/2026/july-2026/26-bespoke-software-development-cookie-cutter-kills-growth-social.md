🧊 Every company hitting €5M revenue hits the same wall.

The SaaS tools that got you there start working AGAINST you.

CRM can't handle your custom pipeline.
PM tool can't model your delivery workflow.
Analytics can't connect to your data sources.

You're forced to choose:
→ Reshape YOUR business to fit the software
→ Build software that fits YOUR business

Companies choosing #1? Plateau.
Companies choosing #2? Scale.

But "bespoke is scary" — so let's bust 5 myths:

**Myth 1:** "Only for enterprise companies"
❌ A 15-person startup with a proprietary algorithm has a STRONGER case than a 5,000-person company running standard processes.

**Myth 2:** "Always over budget"
❌ Projects with clear requirements: 82% on-time.
Projects with vague specs: 31% on-time.
It's a requirements problem, not a tech problem.

**Myth 3:** "Becomes unmaintainable legacy"
❌ ALL software becomes legacy. The question is: can YOU fix it?
SaaS legacy = you migrate. Bespoke legacy = you evolve.

**Myth 4:** "Need a permanent team to maintain"
❌ Maintenance = 5-15% of build cost/year.
That's €1,500-€6,000/month offshore.
Comparable to SaaS subscriptions — but you OWN the asset.

**Myth 5:** "Off-the-shelf is faster"
❌ Faster to INSTALL. Not faster to MAKE WORK.
Off-the-shelf + config + integrations + workarounds = 10-26 weeks.
Bespoke to full productivity = 8-12 weeks.

Your process IS your moat.
Don't let cookie-cutter software kill it.
