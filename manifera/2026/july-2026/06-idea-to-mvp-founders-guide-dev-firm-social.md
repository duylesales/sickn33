🚀 **The MVP playbook nobody teaches in startup school:**

You validated the problem. Talked to 50 customers. Now you need to actually BUILD the thing — and you don't code.

Here's the 5-step process that works:

📝 **Step 1: Product Brief (NOT requirements doc)**
→ What problem? (1 sentence)
→ Who's the user? (1 persona)
→ What are 3-5 core features?
→ What's your success metric?
→ Keep it 2-3 pages. That's it.

🔍 **Step 2: Evaluate 3-5 dev firms**
→ Do they ask about your BUSINESS MODEL? ✅
→ Do they challenge your assumptions? ✅
→ Can they explain trade-offs? ✅

💡 **Step 3: Paid discovery (€2K-5K, 1-2 weeks)**
→ Wireframes, architecture, detailed scope
→ This saves 30-40% of total cost by catching gaps early

🏗️ **Step 4: Build in 2-week sprints**
→ Working software after sprint 1, not slide decks
→ YOUR JOB: show up to every review, test everything

🎯 **Step 5: Launch to 50-100 users, watch what they DO**
→ Not what they said in interviews. What they actually do.

Budget reality check:
Simple MVP: €15K-30K (4-8 weeks)
Medium MVP: €30K-60K (8-14 weeks)
Complex MVP: €60K-120K (14-24 weeks)
