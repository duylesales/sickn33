🚀 For 20 years, the rule of Offshore Dev was: "Trade cheaper hourly rates for higher communication friction."

In 2026, AI and Telepresence are collapsing that "friction tax" to zero.

The fully co-located onshore engineering team is becoming a legacy luxury. The standard is now the **AI-Empowered Hub-and-Spoke Model.**

📍 **The Hub (Europe):** Product Owners, Fractional CTOs, UX Research. Close to the customer and the capital.
📍 **The Spoke (Vietnam):** Elite Cloud Architects, SDETs, and Senior Devs orchestrating AI agents. 

**The AI Shift:** We don't hire offshore teams to type syntax anymore. AI writes the boilerplate. We hire offshore teams to architect resilient systems, manage Kubernetes, and enforce Zero Trust security.

The psychological distance between "Client" and "Vendor" is dissolving. High-fidelity async tools and AI context-translation mean your team 10,000km away feels like they are on the next floor of your building.

If you are paying an agency just to type code, you are wasting your budget. Pay them to engineer.
