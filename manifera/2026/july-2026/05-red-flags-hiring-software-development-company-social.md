🚩 **7 warning signs your dev partner will waste your budget:**

After reviewing hundreds of failed vendor relationships, these patterns predict failure with scary accuracy:

1️⃣ **They quote a price after a 30-min call.** Real estimates require a discovery phase. "€25K for your app" without understanding your requirements = they're guessing or planning to invoice change orders later.

2️⃣ **Sales can't explain the tech stack.** If your contact deflects every technical question with "our engineers will handle that," run.

3️⃣ **They can't show running code.** Screenshots prove nothing. Ask for a LIVE DEMO of a product in production.

4️⃣ **Suspiciously cheap.** When one firm quotes €40K and another quotes €12K for the same scope — the €12K firm is using junior devs, no code reviews, and possibly subcontracting.

5️⃣ **"We're very flexible" on change management.** Translation: no process = scope creep = your budget explodes.

6️⃣ **They want to own YOUR code.** If the contract says "license" instead of "full IP transfer" — you're locked in forever.

7️⃣ **Zero visibility.** No Jira board access, no Git access, no weekly demos = flying blind.

Spot 2+ of these? Find another partner. Immediately.
