🧱 **A rushed MVP architecture will become a massive financial liability when you scale.**

Startups often "move fast and break things," but when enterprise clients onboard, that fragile foundation collapses under the weight of scaling costs.

Avoid these 3 critical SaaS architecture mistakes:
❌ **Single-Tenancy Servers:** Spinning up a new database for every new client means your AWS bill scales linearly with revenue. Use Row-Level Security to share a database securely.
❌ **Hardcoded Permissions:** Hardcoding "Admin" and "User" roles destroys flexibility. Build dynamic, Attribute-Based Access Control (ABAC) from Day One.
❌ **The Unstructured Monolith:** When billing code directly alters inventory data, fixing a bug breaks the whole system. Build a "Modular Monolith" with strict API boundaries.
