🔥 **The SaaS trap nobody talks about:**

Your team spends €40/month on a project management tool.

Sounds cheap, right?

But they spend 11 hours/week copy-pasting data between 3 tools that don't talk to each other.

That's €28,600/year in hidden labour costs. Per person. 💸

Here's when "affordable SaaS" becomes the most expensive software in your stack:

⚡ Your core workflow differs >30% from industry standard
⚡ You connect 5+ systems that don't natively integrate
⚡ Your total SaaS spend exceeds €80K/year for ONE workflow
⚡ You process EU citizen data and need data residency guarantees
⚡ You expect 10x growth in 24 months

If 3+ apply → custom software delivers higher long-term ROI.

The smartest companies don't choose SaaS OR custom.

They build hybrid:
→ Keep SaaS for commodity functions (email, HR)
→ Build custom for competitive moats (proprietary algorithms, unique workflows)
→ Connect them via an integration layer THEY own

This reduces total software spend by 30-40%.

Martin Fowler said it best:
*"If software doesn't provide competitive advantage, don't build it."*

Build where it matters. Buy where it doesn't.
