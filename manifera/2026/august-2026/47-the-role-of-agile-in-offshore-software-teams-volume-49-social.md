🕰️ **How do you run a Scrum team when half the developers are asleep?**

Traditional Agile assumes everyone is in the same room. When scaling an offshore team across time zones, tapping someone on the shoulder is impossible.

How CTOs execute "Distributed Agile":
📹 **Asynchronous Communication:** Stop waiting for the morning standup. Record a 5-minute Loom video explaining the architecture before you log off. The Asia team watches it and codes immediately.
📋 **Hyper-Detailed Tickets:** Enforce a strict "Definition of Ready". If a Jira ticket lacks Acceptance Criteria or Swagger APIs, the offshore Tech Lead automatically rejects it.
🤖 **CI/CD Automation:** The offshore team merges code at the end of their day. The automated pipeline tests and deploys it instantly, so it's ready for European UAT the next morning.
