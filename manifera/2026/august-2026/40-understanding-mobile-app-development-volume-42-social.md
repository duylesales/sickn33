📱 **B2C Mobile Apps and B2B Mobile Apps are entirely different beasts.**

If a consumer app loses Wi-Fi, it shows a cute dinosaur. If a B2B app freezes while a field technician is deep in a concrete basement, the company loses money.

The pillars of Enterprise Mobile Architecture:
📶 **Offline-First Data:** Use robust local databases (Realm/SQLite). The app must save data locally without Wi-Fi, and sync to AWS silently the second 4G returns.
🔒 **MDM Security:** Integrate with Microsoft Intune. Require biometrics (FaceID) to open the app, block copy-pasting corporate data, and implement remote "kill pills" if an employee is terminated.
🧩 **Micro-Apps:** Don't cram the whole ERP into one app. Build a highly focused "Scanner App" for the warehouse and an "Approval App" for executives, all sharing the same backend APIs.
