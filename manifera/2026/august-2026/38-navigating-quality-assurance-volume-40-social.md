📉 **"We wrote 500 new automated tests this month."**

This is a vanity metric. Writing 500 bad tests just slows down your CI/CD pipeline and increases maintenance debt. 

The 3 QA Metrics that CTOs should actually track:
🪲 **Defect Escape Rate (DER):** What percentage of bugs were found by paying clients in production vs. found by your QA pipeline? Elite teams keep DER below 5%.
❄️ **Test Flakiness Index:** If a developer has to click "Re-run" 3 times for a test to pass, they will eventually ignore a real bug. Quarantine flaky tests immediately.
⏱️ **Pipeline Execution Time:** Speed is a feature. If your automated test suite takes 4 hours to run, your developers lose momentum. Parallelize tests in the cloud to run in under 15 minutes.
