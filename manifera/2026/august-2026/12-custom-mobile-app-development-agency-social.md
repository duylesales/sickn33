When evaluating a **custom mobile app development agency**, most companies make the mistake of judging them entirely on their graphic design mockups. But UI design is commoditized. What you must evaluate is their engineering discipline. 

If you want to avoid partnering with a "feature factory" that builds spaghetti code, CTOs must run this 4-question technical interrogation:

1️⃣ **"How do you handle offline states?"** 
👉 Look for local caching (SQLite) & background sync queues. Infinite loading spinners when the 5G drops are unacceptable.
2️⃣ **"How do you manage complex state?"** 
👉 If they suggest bulky Redux for everything, they are living in 2020. Elite teams use Zustand + React Query.
3️⃣ **"How do you deploy the app?"** 
👉 If they manually upload to App Store Connect from a laptop, run away. Demand automated Fastlane CI/CD to protect production certificates.
4️⃣ **"How do you secure the API?"** 
👉 Listen for SSL Pinning and Backend-for-Frontend optimization to prevent payload bloat.
