🛑 **"Build vs Buy" is the wrong question. It assumes all software is just an operational expense.**

If a process is a commodity (payroll, email), you BUY it. Never build a bespoke payroll system. 

But if a process is your competitive moat (a unique pricing algorithm, a proprietary triage workflow), you MUST build **bespoke software solutions**. 

If you mold your core differentiating business process to fit an off-the-shelf SaaS tool, you are mathematically guaranteeing that your operations will never be better than your competitors. Because they can buy the exact same software.

Beware the "Customizable SaaS" trap. Spending €200K to customize Salesforce means you paid for custom development, but you don't own the code, you don't own the IP, and you are locked into their licensing fees forever.
