🏗️ **The architectural decisions you make in Month 1 will dictate your SaaS profitability in Year 10.**

If you choose the wrong database tenancy, your AWS costs will outpace your revenue. 

The strategic B2B SaaS decisions every CTO must face:
🧩 **Monolith vs Microservices:** Don't build 50 microservices on Day 1. Build a "Modular Monolith" to find Product-Market fit, then break it apart when you actually hit scaling bottlenecks.
🗄️ **Database Tenancy:** Dedicated databases for every client is financially catastrophic. Use a single massive database with PostgreSQL Row-Level Security (RLS) to guarantee strict data isolation.
🔌 **API-First Design:** Don't render HTML on the backend. Build secure REST APIs so Fortune 500 clients can integrate your SaaS directly into their ERPs.
