🛑 **"We can't do Continuous Deployment because QA takes too long."**

This is the biggest myth in software engineering. QA is only a bottleneck if it is manual.

Myths vs Facts of Enterprise QA:
❌ **Myth:** QA slows down development.
✅ **Fact:** Automated Playwright pipelines run 5,000 tests in 4 minutes. Developers actually write code 5x faster because they have a safety net and stop double-guessing themselves.
❌ **Myth:** The goal is 100% test coverage.
✅ **Fact:** Chasing 100% coverage wastes budget. Elite CTOs use "Risk-Based Coverage"—mandating 100% on the billing algorithms, but accepting lower coverage on the "About Us" page.
