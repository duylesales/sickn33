🧠 **Enterprise AI is not a "magic brain." It is a Data Engineering problem.**

Buying an OpenAI API key and telling your employees to "prompt better" is a recipe for hallucinations and massive GDPR violations.

How CTOs architect secure B2B AI solutions:
🗄️ **RAG Architecture:** Don't fine-tune models. Use Retrieval-Augmented Generation. The AI searches your private Vector Database for the exact corporate document, eliminating hallucinations.
⚙️ **Data Ingestion Pipelines:** A RAG system is useless if the data is outdated. Use Kafka to automatically chunk and vectorize SharePoint documents the millisecond they are updated.
🛡️ **The Security Gateway:** Never send raw PII to public APIs. Route traffic through an AI Gateway that automatically redacts sensitive data and routes simple queries to cheap, local models.
