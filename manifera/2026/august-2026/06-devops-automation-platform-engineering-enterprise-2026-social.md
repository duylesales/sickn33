The answer: your developers spend 30% of their time on "DevOps toil" — environment provisioning, config management, debugging pipelines — instead of building product.

📊 The DevOps Maturity Model:

**Level 1 — Manual Ops (15% of enterprises)**
SSH deployments. Ticket-based infra changes. Prayers as rollback strategy.

**Level 2 — CI/CD Implemented (50% of enterprises)**
Automated pipelines exist. 3-5 deploys/week. Still bottlenecked.

**Level 3 — Platform Engineering (25%)**
Internal Developer Platform. Self-service environments. Devs don't need to understand K8s.

**Level 4 — DevEx as Product (10% — the elites)**
50-100x more deployments. 1-3 days from new hire to first prod deploy.
