💸 **The most dangerous psychological trap in tech? The "Fixed-Price" software quote.**

When Founders ask, "What is the **cost of making an app**?", they want certainty. When an agency guarantees a cheap, fixed price (e.g., €85,000), cognitive resistance drops. You sign the contract.

But the moment you sign, the agency's goal shifts from *building a great product* to *protecting their margin*.

What happens next?
❌ You ask to change a workflow based on beta feedback.
❌ The agency says: "That's out of scope. That will be an extra €300/hour."
❌ To stay under budget, they skip automated testing and security protocols.

Six months later, your app collapses under the weight of 50 users, and the rewrite costs you €200k.

**The Fix? Time & Materials + Product Discovery.**
Pay for dedicated Agile Sprints. It aligns the engineering team with your business goals, allows you to pivot without penalty, and ensures the architecture is actually built to scale.
