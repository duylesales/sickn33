When you consult an **app development agency in Amsterdam**, be very careful about their bias.

✅ **When to choose React Native:** 90% of the time. If your B2B app is based on forms, dashboards, APIs, and data tracking, React Native is the undisputed king. You cut engineering costs by 40% and deploy to iOS and Android simultaneously. 
✅ **When you MUST choose Native:** Extreme hardware edge cases. If you are syncing real-time data from Bluetooth medical devices, or need complex background GPS tracking while the phone is locked.
