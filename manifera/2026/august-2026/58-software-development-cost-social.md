💸 **"Fixed-Price Agile" is a mathematical contradiction.**

When a CFO asks for the **software development cost** of a new platform, agencies often provide a "Fixed-Price Agile" quote of €150,000. 

This is an illusion. Agile means the scope *must* change based on user feedback. If scope changes, price changes. So how do agencies offer a fixed price?
❌ **The 40% Risk Padding:** They internally estimate €100K, but quote you €140K to cover their financial risk. You overpay for psychological comfort.
❌ **The Change Request Hostage:** When you try to pivot (which is the whole point of Agile), they refuse, citing the contract. You are forced to pay exorbitant hourly rates for "Change Requests."

*"If an agency gives you a fixed price for an Agile project, they are either lying about being Agile, or deceiving you about the price."*
