🚨 **Is your Monolith codebase suffocating your business velocity?**

When you started your SaaS, building a Monolithic architecture made sense. It was fast and easy to deploy.
Today? Every time you try to update the billing module, the entire system crashes. A 5-minute hotfix takes 3 hours of downtime.

You are stuck in the Monolith Trap.

But before you hire a **software applications development company** to fix it, beware of the "Big Bang Rewrite." 
If an agency tells you to freeze all new features for 9 months while they rewrite the code from scratch, FIRE THEM. This almost always leads to catastrophic failure.

Elite engineering teams use the **Strangler Fig Pattern**. 
We extract one module at a time (e.g., Authentication), containerize it with Docker, and route traffic via an API Gateway. Service by service, we "strangle" the old Monolith.
✅ Zero downtime.
✅ Continuous feature delivery.
✅ Total architectural safety.

Don't let legacy code dictate your growth.
