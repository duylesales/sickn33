📱 **Writing UI code is only 30% of a mobile app project. The other 70% is engineering a distribution engine.**

You hired a cheap agency. They built a beautiful mobile app. You launched it.

Three weeks later, a critical bug crashes the app on iOS 18. The agency writes the fix in 4 hours. But they build the `.ipa` manually on a laptop. Apple rejects it for a missing manifest. They fix it. Apple takes 48 hours to review. 

By the time the fix reaches users, 4 days have passed. Your App Store rating is 1.8 stars. Two enterprise clients churned.

The **best mobile app development agencies** don't just build apps. They build pipelines to prevent this exact catastrophe.

**The 3 Pillars of Mobile DevOps:**
1️⃣ **Fastlane CI/CD:** Never compile an app on a developer's laptop. Automate cloud builds with encrypted provisioning profiles (Match).
2️⃣ **Over-The-Air (OTA) Updates:** Use React Native + Expo EAS to push JavaScript hotfixes directly to the user's phone, completely bypassing the 48-hour App Store review.
3️⃣ **Feature Flagging:** Never release a feature to 100% of users. Release to 5%, monitor Sentry crash rates, and kill it server-side if it leaks memory.

*"If you cannot push a hotfix to a critical bug in 20 minutes, your mobile app is a liability."*
