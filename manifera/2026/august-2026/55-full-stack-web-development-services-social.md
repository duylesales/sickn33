🛑 **The "Drop-In" Engineering Team is a myth.**

You hire an offshore agency for **full stack web development services**. On Monday, you give them Jira access. "You are senior engineers, start picking up tickets."

By Friday, they have submitted 3 Pull Requests that fundamentally break your core business logic. Your Tech Lead spends the weekend rewriting them. 

Why? Because you bought *Syntax Knowledge* (they know React), but they lack *Domain Knowledge* (they don't know your business).

*"You cannot build the roof if you do not understand the foundation."*

At Manifera, we refuse to let our offshore pods touch a client's core production code on Day 1. We mandate a 30-day "Onboarding Sandbox":
✅ **Week 1:** They must draw an architecture diagram of your system just by reading the code.
✅ **Week 2:** Non-destructive UI bugs to test your CI/CD pipeline.
✅ **Week 3:** Sandbox feature with Test-Driven Development.
✅ **Week 4:** Full integration.

We trade a slow Month 1 for a flawlessly executed, high-velocity Year 1.
