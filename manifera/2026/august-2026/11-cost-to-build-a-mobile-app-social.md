📉 **The biggest financial mistake startup founders make? Budgeting for the app, but forgetting the iceberg beneath it.**

If you are researching the **cost to build a mobile app** in 2026, generic online calculators will tell you "€30,000." That is dangerously misleading. You are budgeting for the paint job, not the foundation.

To survive, you must calculate the **3-Year Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)**. Here is the real breakdown for a mid-complexity B2B SaaS mobile app:

1️⃣ **The Mobile App Build (React Native):** €60,000
2️⃣ **The Submerged Backend API:** €35,000 (The app is useless without a secure backend).
3️⃣ **Cloud Infrastructure (3 Years):** €25,000
4️⃣ **The OS Maintenance Treadmill:** €45,000 (Patching code every time Apple/Google updates their OS so your app doesn't crash).
5️⃣ **Continuous Agile Iteration:** €100k+ (Because an app is never "finished").

**Total 3-Year TCO = ~€275,000.**
