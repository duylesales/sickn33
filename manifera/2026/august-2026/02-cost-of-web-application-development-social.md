💸 CEO: "How much does it cost to build this web app?"
It's the wrong question. Building the MVP is just the down payment. 

If you want to know if your SaaS is financially viable, you must calculate the **3-Year Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)**.

The true **cost of web application development** in 2026 (Mid-complexity SaaS):
1️⃣ **Initial Build (MVP):** €100,000 (Using a premium offshore/hybrid agency).
2️⃣ **Cloud Infra & 3P Tools:** €75,000 (€25k/yr for AWS, Auth0, Datadog).
3️⃣ **Core Maintenance:** €50,000 (Patching zero-days, upgrading React/Node versions).
4️⃣ **Agile Feature Iteration:** €240,000 (Retaining a dedicated offshore pod for 2 years post-launch).

**3-Year TCO = ~€465,000.**

If you hire a cheap agency to build the MVP for €30k, they skip QA automation and architecture. By Year 2, your Cloud costs explode and Tech Debt paralyzes your roadmap. 

Don't optimize for the cheapest MVP. Optimize for long-term survivability.
