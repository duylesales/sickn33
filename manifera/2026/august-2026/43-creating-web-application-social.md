☠️ **The Single Page Application (SPA) era is dying.**

For 10 years, if you built a B2B SaaS, you built a React frontend and a REST backend. We downloaded megabytes of JavaScript to the user’s browser to do the work our servers should have done. 

The result?
❌ A massive "SPA Tax": Building two codebases for every feature.
❌ Duplicated logic: Form validation on the client AND the server.
❌ The "Hydration Problem": Users staring at a loaded page with buttons that don't work because the 500kb JS bundle is still parsing.
❌ Completely ruined SEO.

*"We traded server-side simplicity for client-side chaos, all so we could avoid full page reloads."*

**The New Standard for B2B in 2026: SSR & Hypermedia.**
By adopting Server-Side Rendering with HTMX or Alpine.js, we get the seamless, no-reload experience of an SPA, but with a SINGLE codebase. 

The server is the single source of truth. You delete Redux. You drop your JS payload to 14kb. And you reduce development time by 40%.

At Manifera, we no longer recommend React for standard B2B SaaS. We use HTMX and SSR to cut your Total Cost of Ownership.
