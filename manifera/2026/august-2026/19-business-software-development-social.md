💸 **Are you paying the "SaaS Tax" for software that doesn't even fit your business?**

Every growing COO hits a crossroads: Buy an expensive Enterprise SaaS, or invest in custom **business software development**. 

If you buy a generic SaaS for your core operations, you are forced to change your unique, optimized workflows to match how the software *dictates* you should work. You lose your competitive advantage.

Worse? The financial math.
For a 200-person company, renting a €150/user Enterprise SaaS costs **€1.8 Million over 5 years.** And you don't even own the code!

Building a custom web app with an elite Hybrid Offshore team costs a fraction of that, and it becomes a proprietary capital asset on your balance sheet, massively increasing your company's valuation.

Rule of thumb: Buy software for commodity tasks (Payroll, Email). BUILD software for your "Secret Sauce" operations.
