**The Pain:** You hire a massive IT staff augmentation firm to build a complex InsurTech platform. In the first three months, developer "A" builds the authentication system. In month four, developer "A" is reassigned to another client, replaced by developer "B."

## Frequently Asked Questions

### (Scenario: CTO auditing a legacy codebase) Why does high developer turnover specifically damage software architecture?
Architecture relies on consistent logic. When developers constantly rotate, new developers don't understand the original design patterns. Instead of adapting, they write messy "wrappers" or conflicting logic to bypass the parts they don't understand. Over a year, this creates a deeply fragile, unmaintainable "Frankenstein" monolith.

### (Scenario: VP of Engineering hiring a team) What is the difference between "Staff Augmentation" and a "Dedicated Team"?
Staff Augmentation is transactional: you rent individual coding hands to fill temporary gaps, and the agency can swap them out. A Dedicated Team (like Manifera's Pods) is strategic: you hire a permanent, cross-functional unit (Frontend, Backend, QA) that stays with your project exclusively for the long term, acting as your actual engineering department.

### (Scenario: Founder worried about offshore integration) How do we ensure the Dedicated Team actually feels like part of our company?
We eradicate the vendor-client wall. Our Dedicated Pods integrate directly into your internal communication tools (Slack/Teams), use your Jira boards, and attend your daily stand-ups via video. Because they are permanent, they absorb your company culture and business goals, becoming a true extension of your internal team.

### (Scenario: HR Director reviewing vendor culture) How does Manifera maintain such high retention rates in a competitive market like Vietnam?
We do not operate sweatshops. We enforce premium Dutch management standards: flat hierarchies, extreme psychological safety, continuous learning budgets, and top-tier compensation. We treat our Vietnamese engineers with absolute respect, which breeds intense loyalty and results in industry-leading retention rates.

### (Scenario: CFO analyzing Total Cost of Ownership) Why is a Dedicated Team cheaper in the long run than hiring cheaper freelancers?
Freelancers have a 100% turnover rate. Every time a freelancer leaves, you pay for weeks of zero productivity while a replacement tries to learn the code. A Dedicated Team preserves institutional memory, meaning feature velocity accelerates over time rather than resetting to zero, drastically lowering your 3-year TCO.
