## Frequently Asked Questions

### (Scenario: CEO evaluating vendor risk) Why are software companies in the Netherlands considered safer for outsourcing than companies in Eastern Europe or Asia?
The Netherlands has one of the strictest, most transparent, and pro-business legal frameworks in the world, specifically regarding Intellectual Property and GDPR. When your contract is with a Dutch entity, you are guaranteed that any disputes or IP breaches will be handled under strict EU laws, giving you massive legal leverage that does not exist in purely offshore jurisdictions.

### (Scenario: CISO auditing access controls) How does a Dutch headquarters actually prevent an offshore developer from stealing code?
It is not just a legal threat; it is a technical barrier. Our Dutch Architects act as the ultimate gatekeepers. They implement Zero-Trust CI/CD pipelines. Offshore developers work on secure Virtual Desktop Infrastructure (VDI) and cannot download code to local machines. The Dutch Hub holds the root access, meaning IP theft is mathematically and physically blocked.

### (Scenario: Founder seeking Series A funding) Do investors care if my software is built by a pure offshore agency versus a Dutch-governed agency?
Absolutely. During Due Diligence, investors heavily scrutinize IP ownership and data compliance. If your core platform is built by an unvetted offshore agency with a weak contract, investors will drastically lower your valuation due to the risk. A contract with Manifera’s Dutch entity provides the legal clarity and security guarantees that investors demand.

### (Scenario: CTO planning system architecture) Does having Dutch governance slow down the actual coding process in Vietnam?
No. Once the Dutch Architects define the API contracts, the security perimeter, and the CI/CD deployment pipelines (which takes intense effort upfront), the Vietnamese Pods operate autonomously within that secure boundary. The governance actually speeds up coding, as developers don't have to guess at security protocols; they just focus on writing business logic.

### (Scenario: CFO analyzing TCO) Is partnering with Manifera's Dutch entity more expensive than hiring an agency in Ukraine or India?
The initial hourly rate is slightly higher than a bottom-tier offshore mill. However, the Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) is drastically lower. You are eliminating the massive financial risks of data breaches, GDPR fines, IP theft, and the inevitable requirement to rewrite insecure code. You get premium European insurance combined with highly sustainable Asian execution costs.
