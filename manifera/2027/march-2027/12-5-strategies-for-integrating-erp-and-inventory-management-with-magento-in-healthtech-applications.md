### Strategic Execution Steps

To ensure success, engineering managers should focus on the following core areas:

1. **Optimize Indexing:** Set up automated cron jobs to rebuild Magento indices frequently and prevent front-end lag.
2. **Integrate ERP Pipelines:** Ensure all ERP connections use queue-based middleware rather than synchronous HTTP requests to prevent timeouts.
3. **Implement Varnish Cache:** Use Varnish Cache to serve static pages and product listings with sub-100ms response times.
