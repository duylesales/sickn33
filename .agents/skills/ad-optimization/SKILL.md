# Ad Optimization & Media Buying Skill

## Purpose
Plan high-converting paid ad campaigns, write multi-angle ad copy (Facebook Ads, Google Ads RSA, TikTok Ads), diagnose ad performance metrics (CTR, CPC, CPA, ROAS, CVR), and optimize landing page conversions.

---

## Capabilities & Workflows

### 1️⃣ Ad Strategy & Audience Targeting
- Define Target Audience Personas (Pains, Desires, Objections, Buying Triggers).
- Design Funnel Architectures: TOFU (Cold/Awareness), MOFU (Consideration/Engagement), BOFU (Retargeting/Decision).
- Recommend Budget Allocation & KPI Metrics (Target CAC, Target ROAS, Target CPA).

### 2️⃣ Multi-Platform Ad Copywriting & Creative Angles
- **Facebook / Instagram Ads:** Primary text, Headlines, Link Descriptions using PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solve), AIDA, BAB (Before-After-Bridge), and Hook-Story-Offer frameworks.
- **TikTok & Reels Ads:** Short-form video scripts (0-3s Hook, 4-15s Value/Demonstration, 15-20s Strong CTA & Visual cues).
- **Google Search Ads (RSA):** 15 Headlines + 4 Descriptions structured for high Quality Score & Ad Relevance.

### 3️⃣ Metric Diagnosis & Optimization Matrix
When presented with campaign data, apply the following diagnostic matrix:
- **CTR (Link) < 1.0%:** Hook/Creative problem -> Test new visual assets, bold headlines, pattern interrupts.
- **High CPC / Low Ad Relevance:** Audience/Offer mismatch -> Refine targeting or align offer with audience intent.
- **Low Landing Page CVR (< 2.0%):** Friction / Disconnect on Landing Page -> Align Hero Section headline with Ad Copy, simplify form/checkout, add social proof.
- **High CPA / Decreasing ROAS:** Ad Fatigue -> Roll out new Creative Angles, introduce Lookalike/Broad scaling, or refresh offer.

---

## Operational Guidelines
- Always generate 3-5 distinct creative angles for A/B testing (e.g., Pain-point driven, Social proof, Curiosity/FOMO, Value/Logic, Emotional transformation).
- Never use vague buzzwords; focus on specific outcomes, numbers, and concrete customer language.
- Ensure 100% Message Match between Ad Copy and Landing Page Headlines.
